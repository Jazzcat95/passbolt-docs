:::danger
If you are changing your domain from **HTTP to HTTPS**, you will unlink the browser extension of all the users.
Before changing the domain, you must ensure that all the users have a copy of their private key to
[recover their account](/user/quickstart/browser/setup-on-another-machine/).

**Pro tips:** In order to download their recovery kit, users can follow this [dedicated guide](/user/basic-features/browser/download-recovery-kit/)
:::
